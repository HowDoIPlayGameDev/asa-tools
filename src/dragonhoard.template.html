What this calculator does and doesn't tell you

Tells you: probability and expected count of each item in dragon hoard crates, using every weight and blueprint chance from the actual server-side loot definition.

Doesn't tell you: what quality (Primitive to Ascendant) the rolled item will be. Quality is controlled at spawn by the Drakeling's XP, level, and biome buff. That mapping lives in blueprint code, not data.
