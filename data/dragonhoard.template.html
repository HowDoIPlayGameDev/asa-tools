What this calculator does and doesn't tell you

It tells you: the probability and expected count of each item appearing in dragon hoard crates. These come from the server's actual loot definition — every weight, every blueprint chance.

It doesn't tell you: what quality (Primitive / Ramshackle / Apprentice / Journeyman / Mastercraft / Ascendant) the rolled item will be. Quality is controlled at spawn time by the Drakeling's stored XP, level, and biome buff — that mapping lives in blueprint code, not data, so it would need empirical testing to derive.

How a crate is composed: every hoard always rolls 5 stacks:

  • 1× quality item: weapon, armor, or saddle (one is chosen)
  • 1× kibble + 1× soup/heal: both always
  • 2× ammo: any 2 of {arrows, bullets, special}

Stack sizes: quality items = 1; kibbles/soups = 1–2; arrows/bullets = 8–12; special ammo = 2–3.
